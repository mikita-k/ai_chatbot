General information about the parking lot:
Our parking lot is open with the following schedule:
- Weekdays (Monday-Friday): 07:00 - 22:00
- Weekends (Saturday-Sunday): 08:00 - 23:00
We provide secure parking with CCTV and lighting.

Pricing and Cost Information:
Hourly rate: $2 per hour. Daily maximum: $20 per day. Monthly passes: available at discounted rates.
Examples of parking costs: 1 hour = $2, 2 hours = $4, 3 hours = $6, 5 hours = $10, 10 hours = $20 (daily max).
For longer periods, the daily maximum of $20 applies.

Location:
The parking is located at 123 Main Street, near the central mall. GPS coordinates: 45.0000, -120.0000.

Booking and Reservation Process:
To make a reservation or book a space, provide your name, surname, car number, and reservation period. After booking, an administrator will review and confirm your request.

Availability:
Real-time availability updates every 5 minutes. Current availability information is maintained separately from this static information.
